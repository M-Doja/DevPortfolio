Coder-Net

 The Problem: Most new programmers lack the skills or experience to get a job right out of school.

The Solution: I worked on a team for Coder Camp to design a social media style application that would help the user be able to get both more skills and possibly experience thru partnered projects, forums, community networking, and resources for jobs and programming. This project was built utilizing the MEAN stack (Angular, Express, Node, and Mongo DB), and designed with Angular Material. I was in charge of implementing file uploads, email verification, backend debugging, layout, styling and functionality of forums and user profile states.

Movie Zone

The Problem:For people who have large collections of movies, remebering what movie you own can be difficult at times.

The Solution: I designed this for a client who needed a way to organize their personal movie collection. The application is very simple, the user simply adds a the movie information and is able to view their personal collection similiar to the IMDB database. This project was built using Javascript, Bootstrap, AJAX, and Firebase for the database.
